My dogs are all mixed breed dogs. They were adopted from local rescue organizations. Cooper is seven, Ruby is six, Carly and Blue are five, and Coal is three years old. The rescue I adopted Cooper and Blue from is a part of Angel Flights. This is our local chapter of Animal Rescue Flights. This service consists of volunteer pilots in Orlando, Florida who fly their private planes on the weekends to pick up puppies from high kill shelters in other states and bring them to Florida where the local rescue organizations can adopt them out. They are saving so many lives!
